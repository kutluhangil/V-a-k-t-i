### Description (EN)
Vakti is a calm, premium space for short, useful tips — each one answering two
simple questions: when, and why.

Two quiet columns:
• Wellness — small everyday habits for digestion, sleep, energy, immunity, skin
  and hydration.
• Communication — supportive phrases for calmer moments with your child.

• Free and ad-free, forever.
• Fully offline — all content ships with the app.
• No accounts, no tracking, no data collected.
• Turkish and English, switch any time.
• Save your favorites, share a beautiful card, add a home screen widget.

Vakti's content is for general information only and is not a substitute for
professional medical, psychological, or parenting advice.
